AboutMe

I am Ranjan kumar. I run a small business Casus artem which involves making logos for heavy metal bands. I started back in january of 2018 when my polish friend Mateusz Sibila ask me to do a logo for his forth coming project called ,"Vishurddh". Since then i might have created more than 150 logos.My speciality is Black metal and death Metal logos, mainly inspired by "True Norwegian Black Metal", "FLorida Death Metal" and "Old School Death Metal" scences.
